Week 2, Day 1

Muscle group(s): Legs

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Back Squat

(9 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3:5 reps @ 60%
  2. Sets 4, 5, 6:4 reps @ 70%
  3. Sets 7, 8, 9:3 reps @ 80%

Exercise 2

Dumbbell Walking Lunge

+ Dumbbell or Kettlebell Goblet Squat

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 5 reps (each leg) Lunge
  2. 10 reps Squat

Superset 2

  1. 5 reps (each leg) Lunge
  2. 10 reps Squat

Superset 2

  1. 5 reps (each leg) Lunge
  2. 10 reps Squat

Exercise 3

Dumbbell Shrug

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3, 4:25 reps
