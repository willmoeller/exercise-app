Week 4, Day 2

Muscle group(s): Shoulders & Triceps

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Barbell Standing Overhead Press

(5 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3, 4:5 reps
  2. Set 5:10 reps

Exercise 2

Dumbbell Front Raise

+ Dumbbell Side Lateral

+ Dumbbell Upright Row

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 10 reps Raises
  2. 10 reps Laterals
  3. 10 reps Rows

Superset 2

  1. 10 reps Raises
  2. 10 reps Laterals
  3. 10 reps Rows

Superset 3

  1. 10 reps Raises
  2. 10 reps Laterals
  3. 10 reps Rows

Exercise 3

Close Grip Bench Press

(6 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3:5 reps
  2. Sets 4, 5, 6:10 reps

Exercise 4

Bench Dip

(3 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3:10 reps

Exercise 5

Band or Cable Pushdown

(3 sets)
