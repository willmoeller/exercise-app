Week 1, Day 5

Muscle groups: Chest & Triceps

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Low Angle Barbell Incline Press

(10 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3:5 reps
  2. Sets 4, 5, 6:8 reps
  3. Sets 7, 8:10 reps

Exercise 2

Dumbbell Press on Flat Bench

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2:10 reps
  2. Sets 3, 4:5 reps

Exercise 3

Dips or Bench Dips

(5 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3, 4, 5:10 reps

Exercise 4

Cable or Band Overhead Tricep Extensions

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3, 4:25 reps
