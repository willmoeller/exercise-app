Week 3, Day 4

Muscle group(s): Chest & Triceps

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Low Angle Incline Press

+ Cable or Band Face Pull

(8 supersets)

Supersets 1-8

  1. 3 reps Press
  2. 15 reps Pulls

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1-4:10 reps

Exercise 3

Cable or Band Overhead Extension

+ Bench Dip

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 20 reps Extension
  2. 10 reps Dips

Superset 2

  1. 20 reps Extension
  2. 10 reps Dips

Superset 3

  1. 20 reps Extension
  2. 10 reps Dips

(3 sets)
