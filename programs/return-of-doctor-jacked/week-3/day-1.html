Week 3, Day 1

Muscle group(s): Shoulders & Triceps

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Seated Dumbbell Overhead Press

(8 sets)

  1. Sets 1, 2, 3:5 reps @ heavy
  2. Sets 4, 5, 6:10 reps @ lighter
  3. Sets 7, 8:20 reps @ lighter (burn)

Exercise 2

Dumbbell Rear Lateral

+ Cable or Band Face Pulls

(4 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 10 reps Laterals
  2. 20 reps Pulls

Superset 2

  1. 10 reps Laterals
  2. 20 reps Pulls

Superset 3

  1. 10 reps Laterals
  2. 20 reps Pulls

Superset 4

  1. 10 reps Laterals
  2. 20 reps Pulls

Exercise 3

Dumbbell Skullcrusher (2 hands on dumbbell)

(10 sets)

  1. Sets 1-10:10 reps

Exercise 4

50 Close Grip Pushups

(As few sets as possible)
