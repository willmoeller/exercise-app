Week 1 Day 2

Muscle group(s): Legs & Back

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Back Squat

+ Dumbbell Pullover

(5 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 10 reps @ 50% Squats
  2. 5 reps Pullovers

Superset 2

  1. 8 reps @ 60% Squats
  2. 5 reps Pullovers

Superset 3

  1. 6 reps @ 70% Squats
  2. 5 reps Pullovers

Superset 4

  1. 4 reps @ 75% Squats
  2. 8 reps Pullovers

Superset 5

  1. 2 reps @ 80% Squats
  2. 8 reps Pullovers
