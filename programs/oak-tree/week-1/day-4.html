Week 1 Day 4

Muscle group(s): Legs & Back

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Deadlift

(7 sets)

  1. Set 1:5 reps @ 60%
  2. Set 2:5 reps @ 70%
  3. Set 3:3 reps @ 75%
  4. Set 4:3 reps @ 80%
  5. Set 5:3 reps @ 85%
  6. Set 6:5 reps @ 75%
  7. Set 7:5 reps @ 75%

Exercise 2

1-Arm Dumbbell Dumbbell Row

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1-4:5 reps

Exercise 3

Snatch Grip Deadlift

(3 sets)

  1. Sets 1-3:5 reps @ 45%

Exercise 4

Rope or Band Face Pull

+ Wide Grip Barbell Curl

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 15 reps Pulls
  2. 15 reps Curls

Superset 2

  1. 15 reps Pulls
  2. 15 reps Curls

Superset 3

  1. 15 reps Pulls
  2. 15 reps Curls
