Week 1 Day 1

Muscle group(s): Legs, Chest, & Arms

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Deadlift

+ Cable or Band Face Pulls

(4 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 5 reps @ 60% Deadlifts
  2. 15 reps Pulls

Superset 2

  1. 5 reps @ 65% Deadlifts
  2. 15 reps Pulls

Superset 3

  1. 5 reps @ 70% Deadlifts
  2. 15 reps Pulls

Superset 4

  1. 5 reps @ 70% Deadlifts
  2. 15 reps Pulls

Exercise 2

Bench Press

(9 sets)

  1. Set 1:5 reps @ 60%
  2. Set 2:5 reps @ 65%
  3. Set 3:5 reps @ 70%
  4. Set 4:3 reps @ 75%
  5. Set 5:3 reps @ 80%
  6. Set 6:3 reps @ 85%
  7. Set 7:5 reps @ 65%
  8. Set 8:5 reps @ 70%
  9. Set 9:5 reps @ 75%

Exercise 3

EZ Bar or Straight Bar Skullcrusher

(5 sets)

  1. Sets 1-5:10 reps

Exercise 4

Band Pull Apart or Dumbbell Rear Lateral

+ Dumbbell Shrugs

(3 sets)

Superset 1

  1. 20 reps Pull Aparts or Laterals
  2. 20 reps Shrugs

Superset 2

  1. 20 reps Pull Aparts or Laterals
  2. 20 reps Shrugs

Superset 3

  1. 20 reps Pull Aparts or Laterals
  2. 20 reps Shrugs
