Week 2 Day 1

Muscle group(s): Back & Arms

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Weighted Pullup

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1-4:4 reps

Exercise 2

Dumbbell Pullover

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1-4:5 reps

Exercise 3

Bodyweight Inverted Row

+ Chest-Supported 2-Arm Dumbbell Row

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 10 reps Bodyweight Rows
  2. 10 reps Dumbbell Rows

Superset 2

  1. 10 reps Bodyweight Rows
  2. 10 reps Dumbbell Rows

Superset 3

  1. 10 reps Bodyweight Rows
  2. 10 reps Dumbbell Rows

Exercise 4

Dumbbell Hammer Curl

+ Dumbbell Shrug

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 20 reps Curls
  2. 20 reps Shrugs

Superset 2

  1. 20 reps Curls
  2. 20 reps Shrugs

Superset 3

  1. 20 reps Curls
  2. 20 reps Shrugs
