Week 2 Day 4

Muscle group(s): Shoulders & Arms

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Barbell Standing Overhead Press

(4 sets)

  1. Set 1:5 reps @ 35%
  2. Set 2:5 reps @ 37.5%
  3. Set 3:5 reps @ 40%
  4. Set 4:5 reps @ 40%

Exercise 2

Dumbbell Front Raise

+ Dumbbell Lateral

+ Dumbbell Rear Lateral

+ Dumbbell Curl to Overhead Press

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 11 reps Raises
  2. 11 reps Laterals
  3. 11 reprs Rear Laterals
  4. 11 reprs Presses

Superset 2

  1. 11 reps Raises
  2. 11 reps Laterals
  3. 11 reprs Rear Laterals
  4. 11 reprs Presses

Superset 3

  1. 11 reps Raises
  2. 11 reps Laterals
  3. 11 reprs Rear Laterals
  4. 11 reprs Presses

Exercise 3

Rope or Triceps Pushdown

(5 sets)

  1. Sets 1-3:10 reps
  2. Sets 4-5:25 reps

Exercise 4

Dumbbell Shrug

+ Triceps Kickback

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 20 reps Shrugs
  2. 10 reps Kickbacks

Superset 2

  1. 20 reps Shrugs
  2. 10 reps Kickbacks

Superset 3

  1. 20 reps Shrugs
  2. 10 reps Kickbacks
