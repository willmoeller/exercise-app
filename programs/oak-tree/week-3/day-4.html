Week 3 Day 4

Muscle group(s): Legs & Back

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Back Squat

+ 1-Arm Dumbbell Row

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 5 reps @ 50% Squat
  2. 5 reps Rows

Superset 2

  1. 5 reps @ 55% Squat
  2. 5 reps Rows

Superset 3

  1. 5 reps @ 60% Squat
  2. 5 reps Rows

Exercise 2

Weighted Pullups

+ Dumbbell RDLs

(3 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 3 reps @ Pullups
  2. 3 reps RDLs

Superset 2

  1. 3 reps @ Pullups
  2. 3 reps RDLs

Superset 3

  1. 3 reps @ Pullups
  2. 3 reps RDLs

Exercise 3

Reverse Grip Barbell Bent Over Row

(3 sets)

  1. Sets 1-3:10 reps

Exercise 4

Alternating Dumbbell Curl

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1-2:5 reps
  2. Sets 3-4:10 reps

Exercise 5

Dumbbell Rear Lateral

+ Dumbbell Hammer Curl

(2 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 15 reps Laterals
  2. 20 reps Curls

Superset 2

  1. 15 reps Laterals
  2. 20 reps Curls
