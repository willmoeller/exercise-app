Week 2 Day 4

Muscle group(s): Legs, Shoulders, & Arms

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Front Squat

(6 sets)

  1. Sets 1-6:6 reps @ 65%

Exercise 2

Barbell Standing Overhead Press

(5 sets)

  1. Sets 1-5:8 reps @ more than week 1

Exercise 3

Dumbbell Front Raise

+ Dumbbell Side Lateral

+ Dumbbell Rear Lateral

+ Dumbbell Upright Row

+ Dumbbell Curl to Press

(2 supersets)

Superset 1

  1. 11 reps Raises
  2. 11 reps Side Laterals
  3. 11 reps Rear Laterals
  4. 11 reps Rows
  5. 11 reps Press

Superset 2

  1. 10 reps Raises
  2. 10 reps Side Laterals
  3. 10 reps Rear Laterals
  4. 10 reps Rows
  5. 10 reps Press

Exercise 4

E-Z Bar Skullcrusher

+ Dumbbell Hammer Curl

(10 supersets)

Supersets 1-10:

  1. 10 reps Skullcrushers
  2. 10 reps Curls
