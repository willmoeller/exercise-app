Week 4 Day 2

Muscle group(s): Legs & Shoulders

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Back Squat

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1-4:5 reps @ 80%

Exercise 2

Barbell Upright Row

(4 sets)

  1. Sets 1-4:10 reps

Exercise 3

Dumbbell Lateral

+ Barbell RDL

(4 supersets)

Supersets 1-4

  1. 10 reps Laterals
  2. 8 reps RDLs

Exercise 4

Seated Dumbbell Press

+ Alternating Dumbbell Curl

(4 supersets)

Supersets 1-4

  1. 8 reps Press
  2. 8 reps Curls

Exercise 5

EZ Bar Skullcrusher

+ Bench Dip

(4 supersets)

Supersets 1-4

  1. 10 reps Skullcrushers
  2. 10 reps Dips
