Week 4 Day 3

Muscle group(s): Back & Chest

Here are a few things to keep in mind for this workout:

  1. All percentages, where listed, indicate the percent of your one rep max. Read more about a one rep max here.
  2. Target rest times between sets and supersets are between 2 and 3 minutes.

Exercise 1

Deadlift

(5 sets)

  1. Sets 1-5:5 reps @ 75%

Exercise 2

Medium Angle Barbell Incline Bench

(5 sets)

  1. Sets 1-5:5 reps @ 65%

Exercise 3

Weighted Chinup

+ Dumbbell Pullover

(4 supersets)

Supersets 1-4

  1. 4 reps Chinups
  2. 6 reps Pullovers

Exercise 4

Barbell Bent Over Row

+ Dumbbell Shrug

(4 supersets)

Supersets 1-4

  1. 8 reps Rows
  2. 10 reps Shrugs
